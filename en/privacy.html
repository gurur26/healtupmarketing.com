5. Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your hard drive by a web page server. Through this method, certain data such as IP address, browser used, operating system, and your internet connection are automatically obtained.

This Website uses Google Analytics, a web analytics service provided by Google Inc. ("Google"). Google Analytics uses cookies to help the website analyze how users use the site. Information generated by the cookie about your use of the Website (including your IP address) will be transmitted to and stored by Google on servers in the United States. Google will use this information to evaluate your use of the Website, compile reports on website activity, and provide other services related to website activity and internet usage.

Necessary, functional, and analytical cookies used by our site are essential for the interface and features to be used as intended; they speed up activities on future visits; they help us understand how visitors use the site and improve content; and under no circumstances do they collect data that would allow us to identify you personally.

6. Cookie Types

This site uses the following cookie types:

# Cookie Type Purpose
1 Necessary Cookies These cookies enable the Website to function correctly and allow you to use all its features.
