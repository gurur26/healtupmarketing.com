What Is HealthUp CRM?

HealthUp CRM is a customer relationship management system developed specifically for the health tourism sector by HealthUp Marketing. It is designed for hospitals, clinics, doctors and agencies to organise, track and optimise patient processes.

Unlike generic CRM solutions, HealthUp CRM is built around the unique dynamics of health tourism — multilingual patient management, country-based segmentation, multi-channel lead tracking and department-based workflows.

A generic CRM isn't enough for health tourism. Tracking a patient's country of origin, lead source, treatment of interest and responsible department simultaneously is only possible with a sector-specific system.

Core Features

Patient Tracking & Management
Manage patient lists efficiently, prevent duplicate entries and maintain data integrity.
Automated Reminders
Scheduled reminders for sales and operations teams — no step gets missed.
Department-Based Database
Separate workflows and patient lists for sales, operations and aftercare teams.
Detailed Reporting
See clearly which source generates the most patients and which channels convert.
Country-Based Segmentation
Analyse which countries generate most demand and shape your strategy by geography.
Staff Performance
Measure which staff performs best on which source; optimise task distribution accordingly.

Direct Contribution to Ad Optimisation

HealthUp CRM is not just a patient tracking tool — it's a decision support system that helps you direct your ad budget as efficiently as possible. Google, Meta, WhatsApp enquiries, organic searches and referral data all come together in a single dashboard.

  • Source-based ROI analysis: See which channel brings real patients and which only burns budget.
  • Real data for campaign optimisation: Sync patient data in the CRM with ad platforms for conversion optimisation.
  • Budget reallocation: Cut spend on underperforming channels; reinforce the successful ones.
  • Referral patient data: Track referrals from satisfied patients and measure the value of this channel.

Who Can Use It?

HealthUp CRM is suitable for institutions of all sizes operating in health tourism. Whether you are a single-physician clinic or a multi-branch hospital chain — the system is configured to your needs.

  • Dental clinics and dental groups
  • Aesthetic surgery centres
  • Hair transplant clinics
  • Ophthalmology and laser centres
  • Check-up and health tourism agencies
  • Multi-branch hospital chains
